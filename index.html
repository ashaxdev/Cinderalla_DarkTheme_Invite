✦ Once Upon a Time ✦

Our Story

👠

They say every great love story begins with a single glance across a crowded room. For Sophia and Alexander, that room happened to be a masquerade ball — where fate, dressed in moonlight and magic, ensured two hearts would find each other before the clock struck midnight.

"Every moment with you feels like magic."

Three years later, beneath a sky full of stars, Alexander knelt down and asked Sophia to be his forever. She said yes before he even finished the question. Now, surrounded by everyone they love, they invite you to celebrate as their fairytale begins its most beautiful chapter yet.
